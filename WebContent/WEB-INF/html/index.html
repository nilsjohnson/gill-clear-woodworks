Gill CC Woodworks specializes in custom made to order wood work. We offer dining room tables, coffee tables, conference tables, bar tops, benches, stools, doors, mantels, hutches, built-ins, cabinets and more. All work is done with sustainably harvested lumber, most of which is harvested by the maker, Sam French, and sawn on site. All pieces are hand made at our shop in Gill Maasachusetts. Let us make custom work for you.
